* 左转

    FD 2B 5D 05 DC 05 D0 05 DC 00

    FD 2B 5D 05 DC 05 DC 06 D0 00

    FD 61 51 00 00 00 00 00 00 00

* 激光开启

    FD 61 51 01 00 00 00 00 00 00
